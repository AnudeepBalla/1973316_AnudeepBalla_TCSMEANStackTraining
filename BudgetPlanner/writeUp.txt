A simple team planner with 3 html pages
1. index.html
2. manager.html
3.finance.html

index.html is the landing page/Home page where the user can chose to be manager or finance. 
based on the selection the page will be redirected. 
Once ur on the manager.html you will have addBudget button to add the entries
once a entry is listed, a dynamic delete button is enabled to delete the entry.

Home button is used to navigate to index.html

in finance.html the table is displayed on clicking the display button.

the total budget is displayed on a card which on hover flips to display the Total Budget amount.
